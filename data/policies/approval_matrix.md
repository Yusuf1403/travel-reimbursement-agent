# Approval Matrix & Authority Rules

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

---

## Approval Thresholds

| Total Claim Amount     | Required Approver         | Auto-Approve? |
|------------------------|---------------------------|---------------|
| $0 – $500              | System (auto-approved)    | Yes           |
| $501 – $5,000          | Direct Manager            | No            |
| $5,001 – $15,000       | Director                  | No            |
| $15,001+               | VP / Finance Head         | No            |

## Escalation Rules

1. **Self-approval prohibition**: Managers cannot approve their own claims above the auto-approve threshold. Their claims are automatically escalated to the next level (Director).

2. **Director self-approval**: Directors' claims above $5,000 must be approved by a VP.

3. **International travel**: All international travel claims, regardless of amount, require at least manager-level review.

4. **Missing documentation**: Claims with missing receipts for expenses above $25 are automatically flagged for manual review, regardless of amount.

5. **Policy exceptions**: Any claim that invokes a policy exception requires VP-level approval.

## Special Categories

| Scenario                           | Required Approver |
|------------------------------------|-------------------|
| Conference registration > $2,000   | Director          |
| Business class flight              | Director          |
| Extended stay > 10 nights          | Director          |
| Client entertainment > $200        | Director          |
| Equipment / supplies > $500        | Manager           |

## Processing SLAs

| Priority       | Target Resolution |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Auto-approved  | Immediate         |
| Manager review | 3 business days   |
| Director review| 5 business days   |
| VP review      | 7 business days   |
