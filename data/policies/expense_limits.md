| Category               | Limit (USD)      | Approval       |
|------------------------|------------------|----------------|
| Registration ≤ $2,000  | $2,000.00        | Manager        |

## Special Rules

- Alcohol is never reimbursable regardless of context.
- First-class airfare is never reimbursable.
- Luxury suites require VP pre-approval.
- Laundry is only reimbursable for trips > 5 consecutive days.
- Tips up to 20% are included in the meal daily limit.
